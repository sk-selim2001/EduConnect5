Contact FAQs

Answers to common questions about getting in touch with us

Our office is open from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM IST. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

For urgent inquiries outside these hours, you can:

  • Email us at support@edupro.in (24/7 response within 12 hours)
  • Use our contact form (responses during next business day)
  • Call our emergency support line at +91 98765 43210 (for existing students only)

We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible:

  • Phone calls: Immediate response during working hours
  • Emails: Within 4-6 business hours
  • Contact form submissions: Within 12 business hours
  • Social media messages: Within 24 hours

For complex inquiries that require research or coordination with other departments, we may need up to 48 hours to provide a comprehensive response.

While we welcome visitors, we strongly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the appropriate staff member is available to assist you. Walk-in hours are limited to:

  • Monday-Friday: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

For specialized services like career counseling or admissions consultations, appointments are mandatory. You can schedule an appointment by calling +91 98765 43210 or using our online booking system.
