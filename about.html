Our Journey

Milestones in educational excellence

2010 - Foundation

Founded in Mumbai with 5 tutors and 30 students, focusing on CBSE curriculum support.

2013 - Expansion

Expanded to 3 cities with 50+ tutors and launched our first online learning platform.

2016 - Recognition

Awarded "Best Emerging Education Provider" by the National Education Council.

2018 - Tech Innovation

Launched AI-powered learning assistant and mobile app with 10,000+ downloads in first month.

2020 - Pandemic Response

Provided free online resources to 50,000+ students during COVID-19 lockdowns.

2023 - National Reach

Now serving students in 25+ cities with 850+ expert tutors and 12,000+ students.
